Edit Appointment

 

  1. Click on the Patient tab.

  2. Enter any available patient identifiers into the search fields and click Search. The results of your search will be displayed on the View Patient screen.

  3. Click on the Appointments tab across the top of the screen. You will be taken to the Patient Appointments screen.

  4. Click on the Delete button to the right of the appointment you wish to edit. A pop up will appear asking you “are you sure you want to delete this record?”

  5. Click on the OK button.

  6. Enter the new appointment details into the required fields.

  7. Click on the Save Appointment button. The appointment will be saved and can be viewed in the Appointments section.
