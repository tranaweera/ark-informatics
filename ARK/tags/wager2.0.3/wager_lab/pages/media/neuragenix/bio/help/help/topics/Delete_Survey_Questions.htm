Delete Survey Questions

 

  1. View the Survey record. (See Search Survey).

  2. Click on the Add/Edit Questions button located below the Survey Description field. You will be taken to the Add Question to “Survey Title” screen.

  3. Highlight the Question you wish to delete and click on the Delete button. A pop up will appear asking you “are you sure you want to delete this record?”

  4. Click on the OK button. You will be taken to the Add questions to Survey Title standard screen.
