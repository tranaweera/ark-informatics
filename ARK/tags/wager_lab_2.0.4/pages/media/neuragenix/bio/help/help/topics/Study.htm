Study Management

The Study module captures and manages the data attributes related to a research study. The data attributes or questions are assembled into Surveys before logically linked to a Study. The surveys are re-useable components for future studies. The following functionality is explained in detail.

  • Create New Study

  • Search Study

  • Delete Study

  • View Ethics Approval

  • Edit Ethics Approval

  • Delete Ethics Approval

  • Create Ethics Approval Attachment

  • Search Survey

  • View Survey

  • Edit Survey

  • Create/Edit Survey Questions

  • Delete Survey Questions
