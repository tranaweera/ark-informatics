Create New Appointment

 

  1. Click on the Patient tab.

  2. Enter any available patient identifiers into the search fields and click Search. The results of your search will be displayed on the View Patient screen.

  3. Click on the Appointments tab across the top of the screen. You will be taken to the Patient Appointments screen.

  4. Enter the Patient and Appointment Details into the Required Fields.

  5. Click on the Save button. The appointments details will be saved and displayed in the Appointments Field.

Appointment reminders will be mailed out if the Email Alert Service is active. The reminder will be sent to the Notify email address on the Notify date.
