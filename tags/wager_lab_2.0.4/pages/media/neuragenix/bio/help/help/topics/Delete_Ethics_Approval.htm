Delete Ethics Approval

 

  1. View the study record (See Search Study).

  2. Click on the Ethics Approval link. You will be taken to the Ethics Approval for Study screen.

  3. Click on the Delete button to the right of the appropriate approval. A pop up will appear asking you “are you sure you want to delete this record?”

  4. Click on the OK button. You will remain on the Ethics Approval for Study screen where the approval will no longer be displayed.
