Create New Biospecimen

 

  1. Click on the Patient tab.

  2. Enter any available patient identifiers into the search fields and click Search. The results of your search will be displayed on the screen.

  3. Click on any component of the patient’s details. The patient’s details should be displayed within the View Patient screen.

  4. Click on the Biospecimen link listed at the top of the screen. This will take you to the Search Results screen. The patient’s biospecimens should be displayed.

  5. Click on the Add New Biospecimen link.

  6. Enter the required Biospecimen ID and any additional information available, into the appropriate fields.

  7. Click on the Save button, located at the bottom of the Add Biospecimen screen. The information entered will be saved. Enter any further sub-type specimen details and click Save. The screen will return to View Biospecimen.
