Completing a Survey

 

  1. Click locating a patient’s survey (See Search Patient's Survey) click Enter next to the survey you wish to complete. You will be taken to Survey Name screen.

  2. Enter the appropriate details into the fields provided. Use the drop down menus when it’s applicable.

  3. Click on the Next tab at the bottom of the screen. You will be taken to the next survey question listing.

  4. Once you have completed all the survey questions, click on the Submit & Complete button at the bottom of the survey screen.

  5. A link message will be displayed 'Survey complete. Click here to return to the patient survey list.' Click on the link and you will be returned to the list of surveys.
