Create Patient Consent

 

  1. Click on the Patient tab.

  2. Enter any available patient identifiers into the search fields and click Search. The results of your search will be displayed on the View Patient screen.

  3. Click on the Consent link across the top of the screen. You will be taken to the Patient Consent screen where an overall list of Studies Consented to will be displayed.

  4. Click on the Study drop down box, and highlight the relevant study.

  5. Enter the consent details and Click on the Save Consent button. Consent details will be saved and displayed on a separate line.

  6. To attach a signed consent form, click on the Add link next to the newly created consent record. You will be taken to the Add Consent File screen.

  7. Click on the Browse button. You will be taken to the relevant file directory.

  8. Select the relevant consent form and click on the Open button. The directory path will be recorded in the Select Signed Consent Form field.

  9. Click on the Save File button. You will be taken back to the Patient Consent screen.
