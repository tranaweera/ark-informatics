Complete an Advanced Search

 

Select Search Domain

  1. Click on the Search tab.

  2. Click on the Select Domain drop down arrow.

  3. Click on Domain you wish to search under.

  4. The screen will reconfigure to coordinate with the particular domain search.

Select Fields and Specify Conditions

  1. Click on the 1st Field Name you wish to limit your search to.

  2. Using the Operator and Priority drop down options, define the range you want to search within.

  1. Operator relates to ‘greater’ or ‘less than’, ‘like’ (similar to), ‘equals’, or ‘! =’ (Not ‘e.g.’)

  2. Priority requires you to designate the desired numerical range or related description.

  1. If you prefer to have Two Search Range Options; within one particular Field Name, select And/Or in the second Connector drop down menu.

  1. If you wish to select all of the Field Names, click on the Check All button at the bottom of the screen.

  2. To unmark all the Field Names, click on the Clear All button at the bottom of the screen.

  1. To perform a search using Multiple Field Names, click on the additional Field Names, and complete the above search details for each.

  2. For every additional Field Name, ensure you have selected And/Or in the first Connector drop down option of each Field Name.

  3. When all the required details have been entered, click on the Submit button at the bottom of the screen.

  4. You will be taken to the Search Results screen where the search results will be displayed.

  1. To alter the number of records you wish to view per screen, enter the Number You Prefer To View and click on the Set button.

  2. To view addition pages with result listings, click on the appropriate Arrow determining the direction to search.

  1. To download a file, click on the Download option at the bottom of the screen.

  1. Right Click on the link and select Save Target As.
