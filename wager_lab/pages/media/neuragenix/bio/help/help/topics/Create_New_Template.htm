Create New Template

 

  1. Click on the Integration tab.

  2. Click on the Create New Template option listed on the menu bar.

  3. Enter the New Template Name into the required field.

  4. Select the appropriate Domain Name from the drop down menu.

  5. Click on the Next button beneath these tasks.

  6. You will be taken to the New Survey Results Template screen.

  7. Select the appropriate Study Name, Survey Name and Template Structure.

  8. Click on the Next button at the bottom of the screen.

  9. You will be taken to the Upload File screen.

  10. Enter the File Name, or, click on the Browse button to find the file.

  11. Click on the Submit button at the bottom of the screen.
